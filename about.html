So, what's all this about?

Ever wanted to add a few cool interactive aspects to your builds but tutorials don't quite catch your attention? Are wiki pages with more information than you need at the moment not really your cup of tea?

The goal of this site is to provide a resource for those who only want quick, bite-sized bits of information about redstone components and mechanics, either as an introduction to the field as a whole, or to learn just enough to build a simple circuit to complete a build.

The guides are primarily written for Java Edition.
